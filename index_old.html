How to Apply SPIKY Wax

01

Scoop & Warm

Begin with a small, pea-sized amount. Rub between palms to warm and emulsify the wax until smooth and pliable.

02

Apply Evenly

Distribute thoroughly through dry or slightly damp hair. Work from roots to tips for complete coverage and foundational hold.

03

Sculpt Your Look

Define spikes, build texture, or craft your signature messy style. Layer more product if needed for enhanced hold and definition.
